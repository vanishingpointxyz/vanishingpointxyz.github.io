Vector.

- Plug and Play Virtual Production solution -

Vector is our flagship product. A combination of hardware elements and custom software that allows you to use cutting edge Virtual Production tools right away, out of the box. We have designed it from the ground up to make it simple to use, and to bring camera tracking, prop tracking and real-time compositing into your studio quickly and easily, without writing any code. Plug in the capture card, plug in the sensor and start shooting.

Full feature demo:

 

 

Vector Hardware/Software package includes:

USB 3.0 hardware sensor with mounting thread. Easily attached to your SDI/HDMI camera, or a virtual camera rig. Includes 20m fiber-optic extension cable. (50m available on request).

PCI capture card to stream video into the real-time composite.

Robust tracking algorithms to localize the camera in 3D space for markerless camera tracking, indoor or outdoor.

Quick start tracking with no need for pre-survey or prior mapping. Launch the software, and start tracking with one click.

Physical marker integration to track simple props or to map a more complex object.

Interactive lens calibration tools to quickly calibrate your camera, with any lens.

Automatic Lens offset tools to get you set up quickly, and remove manual error from the camera-to-sensor offset calculation.

Automatic floor plane detection with lineup tools to easily integrate the virtual into the real world.

Area mapping to scan a space and export to file, for use as post-vis geometry, or lighting/holdout masking in 3D space.

Unreal Engine plugin with live tracking of camera and markers, chroma keying / color correction / matte controls, prop or light constraining to tracked markers, time syncing functions and a HUD with timecode and slate information.

Maya plugin with live tracking of camera and markers, recording to keyframes and auto-naming of recorded takes from slate.

Third-party Plugins:

We supply plugins to connect to Vector, grab the streaming data and apply it to cameras or props in your production software. Currently we ship plugins for Maya and Unreal engine, and will support Unity, Motionbuilder and Lumberyard in the coming weeks. If you use another application, let us know! We aim to support as many third-party display engines as possible.
